- Darker than Black is a text based "chose your own adventure game"
- upon hitting the start button, text will begin to scroll up (star wars style) introducing the user to the story.
- the user will then be prompted to chose their stats.
- the game will use these stats during the adventure to augment story elements.
- The game will then begin.
- At points during the story the user will be prompted to make a choice that will branch the path of the story.
- Audio elements will also be present and will be tied to events, either user choice or location in the story.
- animation elements will also me preset, although their implementation as of this time is TBD.
